# Bracelets Product Short Description Enhancement Guidelines
Enhance Bracelets Product Short Description by following below-mentioned guidelines:
- Write 1 paragraph in active voice with no bullet points.
- Minimum 80 words, ideally 100 words.
- Include Product Name.
- Use related keywords that would help in Search Engine Optimization.
- Avoid promotional text, special characters, prohibited words, puns and mention of retailers other than Walmart.

## Example:
Get on board with the utility trend in Celebrity Pink's Boiler Suit.
Available in super soft twill or stretch denim, this jumpsuit has a
relaxed fit with an elastic back waistband so it's fitted in all the
right places. Best of all, your outfit is complete with just one piece!
